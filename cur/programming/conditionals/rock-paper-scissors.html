For this activity, you will create the Rock, Paper, Scissors game.

Step 1:

First, make a script that makes the cat and the duck say either Rock, Paper, or Scissors when the green flag is pressed.


Exercises

  • Add a third character. After the duck and the cat pick their choice and say it, the third character should say who wins. You may find this chart useful.
  • Add cat score and duck score variables that keep track of how many times each character has won. You can make these variables visible on the stage by clicking on the check-box next to the variable name.
