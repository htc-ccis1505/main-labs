Practice Problems for Fundamentals of Programming

The idea here is that in only 20 hours of practice, you can be proficient at what ever it is you want to learn - if you plan and use your time well.

The following are intended to be 30 minute long practice sessions for you to use to become proficient with Snap and programming logic. They are grouped and ordered such that they correspond to the material found in the labs and topics covered in class.
