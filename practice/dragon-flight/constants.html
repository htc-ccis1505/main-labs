Variables for Constants?

While it may not sound like it makes a lot of sense, we often use variables for values that we don't expect to change while the program is running. These variables are called constants and they are often given names in all capital letters. Our max and min speed make great constants.


If you didn't make variables for the min and max, update your script to do so.
