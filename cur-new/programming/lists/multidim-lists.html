Multi-Dimensional Lists

Throughout the course you will find many uses for lists. We can have lists of words, such as those from the previous example. We can also have lists of numbers, perhaps to keep track of your grades in this course.

But we can also used lists to store more complex data by using a list as an item inside another list. We might use a list to represent a sprite's position as a single pair containing the X and Y position numbers, and then collect a list of those pairs to move the sprite or to draw a graph.
