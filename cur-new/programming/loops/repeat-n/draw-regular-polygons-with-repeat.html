A regular polygon is a shape in which all the sides are the same length and all the turning angles are the same. If the sides are different lengths or if the angels are different, then it is called an irregular polygon. A square is a regular 4-sided polygon, but a rectangle is an irregular polygon.

Use what you learned from drawing a square and use the to draw the following regular shapes:
- Equilateral Triangle
- Pentagon (5 sides)
- Hexagon (6 sides)
- Octagon (8 sides)
