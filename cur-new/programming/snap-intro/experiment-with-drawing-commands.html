Try to get comfortable with the blocks under the Motion tab and the Pen tab. Figure out what each one does, and try to use these blocks to draw a square or a simple picture. Get started by testing these blocks:

Tips and Tricks:

  1. Once the pen is down, it stays down even in a different script. Use the pen up block to lift the pen to stop drawing.
  2. To show the direction and the x and y position of the sprite, use the reporters from the bottom of the Motion palette. You can set these to be shown on the stage by clicking the check-box next to them, as described in the Reporters activity you saw earlier in the lab.
